Start your business

Pick the right structure.

Four ways to incorporate in India. We help you choose, then handle the filing end to end.

Most popular

Private Limited

Best for startups raising capital and issuing ESOPs.

  • Separate legal entity
  • Equity and ESOP ready
  • Preferred by investors
Solo founder

OPC

One person company for a single founder who wants limited liability.

  • One shareholder
  • Limited liability
  • Lower compliance load
Partners

LLP

Limited liability partnership for services firms and small teams.

  • Partner liability capped
  • Flexible profit share
  • Fewer filings than Pvt Ltd
Non-profit

Section 8

For mission-driven organisations and foundations.

  • Non-profit status
  • Tax benefits eligible
  • Grant and CSR ready
